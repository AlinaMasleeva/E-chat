Company Introduction

Beijing Aite World Network Technology Co., Ltd. is an innovative-oriented company using blockchain technology. The company adheres to the principles of the Internet open and transparent culture and makes every effort to help all businesses and individuals in the field of e-commerce in their work. The company unites applicable technologies of the networking, blockchain, financial accounting technologies with the help of virtual currencies and the latest technologies to create an open service platform for instant messaging from around the world.

e-chat platform presentation

E-chat is an application of the global social platform for pure communication, developed by Beijing Aite World Network Technology Co., Ltd.

The platform has such functions as real-time communication between users from all over the world, one-touch photo processing, high-quality content and many others.

The site cooperates with Alipay, Wechat, UniionPay, Visa, E-Dinar Coin virtual currency and other popular payment platforms, making it easy to replenish mobile phone balance and make other everyday payments.
